Our Focus

Our focus is on the “logistics” of home healthcare service delivery, specifically on improving the scheduling and delivery of homecare services. We are dedicated to helping homecare providers optimize the delivery of a rapidly growing demand for homecare services against an often challenged availability of qualified care providers to provide the highest quality of care at the lowest cost.

Why Logistics?

Home health care service delivery is a logistics problem. Did you know you were in a logistics business? Do you view it that way? We think you should. In 2009 the National Association for Homecare and Hospice (NAHC) wrote a paper comparing the mileage driven by UPS worldwide to clinicians in the home care industry. They found that UPS drove 2 Billion miles compared to 5 Billion miles home care clinicians drove. That’s more than twice as much as the largest logistics company in the world! And the need for homecare services is only growing.

Logistics in home health care are unique and challenging. Unlike for a UPS or a FEDEX where delivery can be largely at any time and by any personnel, home health care requires precision delivery – the right resource at the right time. This need for a precision has not been effectively met by tools that help agencies provide home care precisely. Rather, agencies are often left to meet their growing demand for patient care against tight resources through very rudimentary means that rely on the expertise and knowledge of scheduling personnel...making the process more art than science, more manual than automated. This leaves much room for improvement.

That's where HCI can help

We’ve developed a breakthrough solution that revolutionizes the scheduling and delivery of homecare service. The homecare service agency can better plan and control the delivery of service against their available service resources. Utilizing Homecare Intelligence, our customers can maximize the delivery of homecare services while minimizing the cost of homecare service delivery.

SCHEDULER TOOLS

Schedulers use the recommendation engine to alleviate the guesswork associated with scheduling and assigning visits to clinicians. The organization’s initiatives and goals can be consistently measured while automation ensures effectiveness and efficiency.

CLINICIAN TOOLS

Clinicians have an intuitive mobile application to manage their daily schedule. Clinicians can view their driving route with turn-by-turn directions, capture and report mileage. Optionally, they can move, cancel and confirm visits, based on security roles.

MANAGEMENT TOOLS

Managers can use a number of real-time reporting and analytical tools to oversee branch operations and take action. Management can use HCI to align their agencies responses to the ever-changing clinical demands of home care.

PRIMARY CLINICIAN / CASE MANAGER

Identify and assign a primary clinician (or case manager) for the patient. The goal is to assign the best clinician that can complete up to 100% of the visits for the patient’s episode to improve continuity of care. You can assign a primary or alternate clinician for each discipline.

RECOMMENDATION ENGINE

The clinician recommendation engine analyzes visit types, distances between patients, clinician availability, patient/staff preferences, and much more to generate optimal clinician assignments.

ASSIGNMENT ALGORITHIMS

HCI utilizes a number of algorithims when evaluating clinicians for visit assignments. The application recommends the best clinician to complete a single visit, or visits for a patient's entire episode.

HCI Auto Contact

The HCI Auto-contact solution automates routine calling tasks to streamline clinician contact. Pre-visit patient confirmation (telephone contact) - Most agencies believe that contacting their patients before the scheduled visit reduces missed visits. Unfortunately, the burden for this initiative falls on the clinicians, reducing their overall satisfaction. Auto-call contacts the patient and allows them to press ‘1’ to confirm the visit or ‘2’ to decline.
